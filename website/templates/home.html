Team 190 is a joint WPI and Mass Academy FIRST Robotics Competition team located at WPI in Worcester, MA. The team is one of the Original & Sustaining FIRST Robotics Competition teams. The team has participated in FIRST every year since 1992.

Team 190 has a rich history of innovation, culture, and spirit that make our team unique. We welcome students from any school or home-school to join our team. Throughout the year, we travel to all different places from libraries and schools, to fairs and assisted living facilities, to showcase our team, talk about the importance of STEM, and bring robotics into the hands of many!
